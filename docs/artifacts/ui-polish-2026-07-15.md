`provisional:true`). No field reordered; append-only.

**Frontend (`C:\Users\Joe\Desktop\swap key\SwapKey-Wallet.html`, outside the repo):**
- Permanent **PRE-ALPHA · TESTNET ONLY · NO REAL FUNDS** banner (both modes) + build version.
- A **live status strip** (hidden in sim mode; shown when a backend answers) rendering,
  from `/status`: manifest version+id (LOUD v0-provisional warning), swaps `active/max`
  (409-at-cap hint), claim posture, and — contextually — the phase-0 pre-onboard warning,
  refund-reality copy, claim-hold copy, prominent alarms, swap history, and (gap-tabled)
  fee weather.
- All server-derived strings rendered via `textContent` (no injection); re-render is
  idempotent; strip hides on backend disconnect. The simulated teaching walkthrough is
  untouched (fallback / teaching layer).

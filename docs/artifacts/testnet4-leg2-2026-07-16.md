  attempt (~50% by design) — the refusal arm was NOT exercised this leg; the
  onboarding-delay refusal arm was exercised 18 times instead (§3).
* **Exact-D privacy invariant held**: both settlement outputs are exactly
  1,000,000 sats; every fee came out of the baked `delta_fee` envelope.
* **Fee-weather preflight (task 26) validated live in BOTH arms** (§5).
* **Claim-delay posture dial worked live**: B held its SL claim for a
  sampled 3-block delay (reveal 144335 → broadcast at 144338), runtime
  ceiling clamp engaged, then confirmed at 144339.
* **Transport (task 24) + ticket flow**: `skt1…` ticket minted, dialed,
  `ticket rendezvous ok` on both sides — twice (the immature attempt at
  02:36 and the winning attempt at 10:38).

## 6. Findings (all non-blocking)

1. **`status` maturity blindness** — an immature unit read `PreEncumbrance/
   Unspent` with nothing explaining the swap refusal. FIXED this session
   (`445be66`: per-unit `[onboarding delay: matures ~<UTC> & height ≥ N]`
   annotation + 4 regression tests; TESTER-GUIDE re-synced; `diag`
   deliberately left redacted — a pasted maturity timestamp leaks the
   decorrelation draw).
2. **Completion rebroadcast every poll tick** while unconfirmed (~50
   identical `completion (re)broadcast` lines over the 2-h block drought).
   Harmless (same txid, idempotent), just noisy — candidate quiet-down for a
   later polish task; noted for task 29's log-growth checklist.
3. **Node restart mid-wait** (07-15 22:15): bitcoind was stopped cleanly by
   the other project sharing it and restarted; wallet processes were not
   running at the time (between retry attempts) so nothing to observe — the
   serve-side reconcile behavior remains task 29's restart-injection item.
4. Onboarding-delay ETA math: testnet4's ~9.2 min/block makes the HEIGHT
   anchor the binding edge (delay/600 assumes 600 s blocks) — worth one line
   in the runbook §2b so nobody watches a wall-clock ETA again.
